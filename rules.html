Rules:

Welcome to Journey Across Europe!
The rules are simple:
1) You start in Helsinki Vantaa Airport.
2) You get a clue where you should go next:
- Right guess:
+150, +100 or +50 points depending on the length of the route.
- Wrong guess:
-75, -50 or -25 points.
- Guess wrong 3 times: game over!
3) Once you get to the correct airport, you get a task:
- Complete the task and get +75, +50 or +25 points!
- Fail the task, shame on you, -37.5, -25 or -12.5 points.
4) Finish the game by arriving to the final airport! :)
